About Spirulina

What is Spirulina?

🌱

Natural Algae-Based Nutrition

Spirulina is a nutrient-rich blue-green algae known as one of the world’s most powerful natural superfoods. It contains high-quality protein, vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and essential amino acids that support overall health and wellness.

For centuries, spirulina has been valued as a natural source of nutrition due to its exceptional health benefits and sustainable cultivation process.

  • Rich in plant-based protein
  • Supports immunity and energy levels
  • Contains antioxidants and essential nutrients
  • Supports muscle recovery and overall wellness
  • Suitable for health-conscious lifestyles
  • Naturally grown with minimal environmental impact

Phycocyanin

Natural Blue Nutrition

Phycocyanin is the valuable blue antioxidant pigment naturally extracted from spirulina. Known for its vibrant natural color and wellness benefits, it is widely used in nutritional supplements, beverages, cosmetics and wellness products.

It is a clean-label, plant-based alternative to synthetic colors and additives.

  • Nutritional supplements
  • Natural food coloring
  • Functional beverages
  • Cosmetic and nutraceutical industries
